RESEARCH PAPER STRUCTURE
1. Abstract — 150-250 words. What, why, how, result.
2. Introduction — Context, problem, your contribution.
3. Literature Review — What exists. What gap you fill.
4. Methods — Reproducible. Rigorous. Detailed.
5. Results — Data. Figures. No interpretation yet.
6. Discussion — What results mean. Limitations.
7. Conclusion — Summary. Future work.
8. References — IEEE or APA format.
Target: arXiv preprint first. Then journal submission.
